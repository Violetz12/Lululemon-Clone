Lululemon: From Yoga Studio to Global Movement

Our Story: A Journey of Innovation

Vancouver, 1998 — Lululemon was founded with a vision to create high-quality athletic apparel, blending function, style, and innovation. What started as a small studio soon became a worldwide phenomenon. Initially focused on yoga, Lululemon’s passion now extends to various fitness forms, ensuring support for every active lifestyle.

Mission: Unleashing Potential

Our Mission: To elevate the world by helping individuals realize their potential. Our goal is to foster well-being and connectedness for a healthier, happier world.

Core Values: What Drives Us

  • Connection: Building meaningful relationships with customers, teams, and communities.
  • Well-being: Inspiring balance of mind, body, and spirit.
  • Innovation: Leading in fabric, technology, and design to enhance performance.
  • Inclusion: Creating a welcoming space for all, embracing diversity.

Community Impact: A Force for Change

Through our Here to Be program, we partner with local organizations to promote well-being through movement and mindfulness. From yoga to workshops, our initiatives aim to make a positive impact.

Why Choose Lululemon?

Lululemon is dedicated to enhancing your active lifestyle with high-quality apparel designed for performance and comfort. Our proprietary fabrics, such as Luon and Nulu, offer exceptional stretch, breathability, and moisture-wicking properties, ensuring you feel great during any activity. We are committed to sustainability, aiming to use 100% sustainable materials by 2030, which reflects our responsibility to the planet. Our community engagement fosters genuine connections through local events and fitness classes, encouraging a holistic approach to health and wellness. With innovative designs and a focus on inclusivity in sizing, Lululemon empowers everyone to find their perfect fit. Choosing Lululemon means embracing a lifestyle that prioritizes quality, community, and well-being.
